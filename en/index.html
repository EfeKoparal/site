self-taught

You must have seen the word self-taught on social media at least once. Self-taught means improving yourself alone. I also believe that the best education for you will be yourself. According to my experience, I think that it is the highest dimension to learn by reading an article or a book and by doing practice.


One of the best benefits of self-taught is self-confidence and courage. The walls of preconception you have created in your mind while learning new information disappear. You learn new things much easier. The reason for this is that you actually learn how to learn a new information rather than learning a new information. The more you gain knowledge in different fields, the easier it will be for you to host a new information in your memory.
